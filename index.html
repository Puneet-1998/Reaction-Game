How to Play
  1. Press Start or click the 3D scene to begin.
  2. Watch the shape — it will turn amber while waiting.
  3. When it flashes green and expands, click or press Space as fast as you can!
  4. If you click before the green signal, it counts as a false start.
  5. Your reaction time is measured in milliseconds. Under 250ms is fast!
